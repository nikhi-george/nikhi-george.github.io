— About

I'm a designer who started in architecture, drifted into interiors, then found a real home somewhere between brand, space and the small stubborn details in between.

The thread through everything I make is the same: pay attention, sweat the proportions, leave room for a quiet wink.

Outside the studio you'll find me hunting for cafés with good light, collecting reference photos of staircases and rereading the books on the shelf below.
